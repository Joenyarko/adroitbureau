About Us

Powering Africa's sustainable industrialization through innovative software solutions

Our History

The company was started as a partnership with the name Adroit Technologies in 2009. At this time the founders were about completing their undergraduate degrees.

In 2014, Adroit Bureau Ltd. was registered as a limited liability company and our flagship product 'Total Accounting' was launched.

By 2021, Adroit Bureau Ltd. evolved into a software holding company with multiple subsidiaries, dedicated to providing appropriate software systems for business productivity suited for African conditions.

Our Vision

To power Africa's sustainable Industrialization by providing appropriate Software Systems for Business Productivity suited for conditions on the continent.

Our Mission

To provide Entrepreneurs and Small and Medium scale businesses with affordable access to accounting and book keeping products and online services thereby increasing their chances of success in a volatile business environment.
